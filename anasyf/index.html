Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about trading on CoreTradeGlobal.

B2B (Business-to-Business) is the exchange of products and services between companies.

Our platform brings these companies together‚Äîwhether a manufacturer is looking for new customers, an importer is seeking a suitable supplier, or a distributor needs to complete stock‚Äîfacilitating search and communication in one place.

RFQ (Request for Quote) is the process where a buyer requests price quotes and commercial terms for a needed product/service. The goal is to receive comparable offers from multiple suppliers to make the right purchasing decision.

The RFQ Process on Our Platform:

  • In the Add Request (RFQ) form: Enter product name, category, target country, quantity, and detailed description.
  • Published RFQs are filtered on the All Requests page (by category/country).
  • Suppliers view your RFQ and send their proposals and documents via secure messaging.
  • You can easily compare the quotes based on country, delivery time, and price breakdowns.

The core advantages our platform offers you are:

  • Global Reach: Get your products and requests in front of buyers and sellers worldwide.
  • Visibility: Highlight your company with banner and featured product advertisements, promoting your product to all CoreTradeGlobal members.
  • Rapid Discovery: Instantly find exactly what you are looking for using category, country, and product filters.
  • Brand Strength: Increase your brand awareness and take your place at the center of the digital world.
  • Secure Trade: Conduct secure commerce with verified profiles and transparent information.

From manufacturers to import-export firms, brand owners to procurement specialists, all businesses that want to be at the center of digital trade can join CoreTradeGlobal.

Starting your B2B trade journey on our platform is easy:
